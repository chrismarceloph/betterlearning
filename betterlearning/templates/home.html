Better Learning

  • Learn it. Prove it. Apply it.

    In Better Learning we offer you a fresh start: A virtual identity in a virtual world setting with real projects to help you acquire real life skills. Draft your own courses or join related groups, classrooms and global communities. Start building with your own hands and see the world not as a mere theoretical concept but as a laboratory, a playground of ideas.

  • Principles for Learners

    1. Learning is by doing.
    2. You are in charge of your own learning.
    3. There are so many ways to learn; be creative.
    4. You are what you do and not your titles, past achievements or failures.
    5. Share your knowledge and skills; don't be greedy.
    6. Always be curious, and always aim to improve yourself.
    7. Be nice to others; don't be a jerk.
    8. Collaborate not compete, that's how the outside world works.
    9. Before asking other people, try Googling it first.
    10. Break all the rules whenever sensible, including this one.
